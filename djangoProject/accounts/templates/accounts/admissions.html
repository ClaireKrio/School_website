Admissions

Admission Information

Welcome to the admissions section. We are excited to have you consider joining our school. Here you will find all the information you need to apply and join our community.

Application Process

Our application process is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to apply:

  • Step 1: Complete the online application form available on our website.
  • Step 2: Submit the required documents, including previous academic records, birth certificate, and proof of residence.
  • Step 3: Schedule an interview with our admissions office. The interview can be conducted in person or virtually.
  • Step 4: Wait for the admission decision, which will be communicated via email.
  • Step 5: Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process by paying the admission fee and submitting the necessary forms.

Please ensure that you complete all steps by the specified deadlines to secure your place in the new intake.

Accessing the Student Portal

Once you have been accepted and enrolled, you will have access to the student portal. The student portal is a one-stop destination for all your academic needs, including viewing grades, managing your schedule, and communicating with faculty.

How to Access the Student Portal

Follow these steps to log in to the student portal:

  • Step 1: Visit the student portal login page at Student Portal Login.
  • Step 2: Enter your username and password, which were provided during the enrollment process.
  • Step 3: Click the "Login" button to access your personal dashboard.
  • Step 4: If you encounter any issues, please contact the IT support team at itsupport@school.com.

If you have not yet received your login credentials, please check your email for the enrollment confirmation or contact the admissions office for assistance.
